This is my most recent work.
It's called:
As Time Goes On:

For this piece I started with the vocals, I noticed that these vocals, which do not originally go together, tell a story.
I decided to put them together to create a sad story of the falling of a relationship, from before it started to fall apart
to after it was all over. I searched for a guitar that would reflect this rise and fall, which is when I landed on what I
have now. this is what I used as a basline, and with the addition of drums and some timing changes to make it more
appealing, we are left with this final product.
